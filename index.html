ABOUT OUR HOTEL

MONO is a seasonal cafe and market featuring fresh baked goods in East Hollywood, California, run by pastry chef/baker Roxana Jullapat and chef Daniel Mattern. As long-time supporters of product-oriented cooking, Jullapat and Mattern established more than 15 years of professional careers in famous Los Angeles kitchens (including AOC, Lucques, Campanile, and Cooks County) before opening MONO. At Friends & Family, they continue to work on handmade food, including handmade breads and pastries made with locally sourced heirloom grains every day, as well as daytime menus.
